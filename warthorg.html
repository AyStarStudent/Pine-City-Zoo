WARTHOG

Warthogs are medium-size, pig-like mammals that are native to Africa. They are known for their distinctive faacial "warts" and curved tusks, which are actually modified canine teeth. Warthogs are herbivores , feeding on various plants, including grasses, roots and fruits. They are found in savanna grasslands, where they live in small groups usually with a dominant female.
