KOALA

Kaolas are marsupials native to Australia, known for their distinctive appearance and diet consisting of mainly eucalyptus leaves. They are arboreal, spenidng most of their time in trees.

Koalas are generally quiet animals but can make loud calls during the mating season. they are considered vulnerable because of the habitat loss and other environmental factors. Visitors to the zoo can observe kaolas in a settings that mimic their natural environment.
