GIRAFFE

Giraffes are the tallest aniamls in the world, with adults reaching up to 18 feet (5,5m) tall. They're herbivorous feeding on leaves, fruits and flowers from trees and shrubs.

HOW HIGH?

Giraffes are found across savannas and grass-lands fin sub-suharan Africa, where they roam in small groups. Their distinctive long necks allow them to reach food that other animals cant, and their spotted coats help hem blend in with the surroundings. Here they are one of the most popular attractions, you can even feed them or take pictures!
