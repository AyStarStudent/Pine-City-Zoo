GEMSBOK

Gemsbok are large antelopes native to South Africa. They are known for their striking appearance, with long straight horns, and a distinctive coat pattern.

ONLY IN THE CAPE

Gemsbok are well adapted to arid environments and can survive without water for a long period of time. In the Zoo the guides will teach you abou the adaptation that allows gemsbok to thrive in harsh desert conditions.
